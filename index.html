Collateral Intake & Classification

Problem

  • Failure to properly identify and register collateral
  • Incorrect jurisdiction registration
  • Missing mandatory details (debtor name, serial-number goods, etc.)
  • Lapses in registration updates
  • Priority rule violations with multiple security interests
  • Compliance issues during restructuring

Consequences

  • Loss of security interest priority
  • Unenforceability against other creditors
  • Major financial and legal liability
